### Explain the inheritance rules for human blood groups

Bloud groupes are determined by three different genes $I^A$, $I^N$ and i. Where $I^A$ and $I^B$ are codominat therefore both equally dominant and $I^A$ and $I^B$ are both dominant to $i$. 

The following genotypes follow from these genotypes

- The phenotype $A$ comes from the genotype $I^AI^A$ or $I^Ai$

- The phenotype $B$ comes from the genotype $I^BI^B$ or $I^Bi$

- The phenotype $AB$ comes from the genotye $I^AI^B$

- The phenotype $0$ comes from the genotype $ii$
