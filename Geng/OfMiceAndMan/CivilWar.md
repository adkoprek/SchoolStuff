# Civil War

The Civil War took place in the early years of the formation of the USA. It took place between the 12th April 1861 to the 26th May 1865. The conflict was about the dispute over whether slavery should be allowed.

`Abraham Lincoln` was the 16th President of the United States, elected in the year 1960. He was against slavery and forbade it in the entire USA. Most of the slaves (3.5M out of 4M) lived at that time in the south. Seven South States were against this decision and formed the `confederation`. The rest of the US created the `Union` that was led by Abraham Lincoln.

The war started with the bombarding of Fort Sumter in South Carolina. And the Confederation lost the war.
