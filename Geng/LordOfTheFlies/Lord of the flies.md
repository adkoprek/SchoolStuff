# Lord of the flies

I choose to write about Piggy because I am able to emphasize with him on some level. When I was younger I was also a little bit overweight and sometimes the other kids made fun of me because of this fact. Furthermore Piggy is percieved differently by the othe rkids because he prefers to use his mind and itellect combined with logical reasoning instead of just following his basic instincts. I can relate to this in the way that I never understood the reason why other kids at my age at this time liked to fight together and play games were the potential for getting injured in a stupid way was high.

Piggy is not working against the protagonist Ralph. He isn't thrilled that Ralph was elected the leader of the boys on the island but he acknowledges it. Piggy has good intensions and instead of working against the protagonist he would rather help him in making the right decisions.

Piggy adapted to living on the island with the other boys in his own way. At the beginning of the book we can remember how piggy was extremely ratinonal and pessimistic about their situation and got unbelivebly frustrated when everyone started to call him Piggy. His frustration just fuled the desire of the other boys to anger him. In fact this is something that I can actually relate to very well. When I was younger and went to primary school I had a very short temper and some other kids took advantage of that. They often angered me just because they liked to see my reaction. And this is something that I had to learn and also Piggy adapted to on the island. To sumerize it, Piggy changed his behaviour to be better accepted in the group of the boys.

Piggy plays an important role in the sotory he provides with his rational ideas a new view on certain thing to the reader and the boys on the island. It is refreshing to hear the story told from an different more objective perspective. I suppose that he has the role of trying to help the boys to make better decision on the iland and give a other side to the reader.

If the beast turns out to be real in the forest Piggy may be potentially wore suited to escape from it because he is not in good shape.
